Rust2D - About Me

Hi there! Welcome to my portpholio! Im Rustam (Rust2D) and im a professional photographer from Moscow! Photography has been my passion ever since I can remember. Now I cannot even imagine my life without it!

In my works i mostly try to focus on the movement aspect of photography. I love capturing people in motion. I believe that those kind of photos convey human emotion the best!
